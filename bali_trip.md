# Bali Trip Plan — 8 Days, Culture-Focused, with a 2.5-Year-Old

> **Travelers:** 2 adults + 1 toddler (2.5 yrs, walking)
> **Duration:** 8 days / 8 nights
> **Style:** Culture > luxury, slow pace, no rushing, no nightlife
> **Status:** Draft v3 — living document, update freely

---

## Trip shape

| Day | Base | Nights | Theme |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1–2 | **South Bali (Jimbaran / Seminyak)** | 2 | Arrival, Uluwatu cliff temple, Nusa Dua beach |
| 3–5 | **Ubud** | 3 | Arts, dance, monkey forest, Bali swing |
| 6–7 | **Sidemen** | 2 | Rice valley, weaving villages, slow days |
| 8 | **Sanur** | 1 | Pre-flight buffer, easy airport morning |

**On the South Bali base:** Kuta is loud and party-heavy. Seminyak is upscale-trendy but has nightlife strips. **For "Uluwatu + Nusa Dua + no nightlife", Jimbaran or Nusa Dua are better picks** — beach-resort vibe, family-friendly, closer to Uluwatu (15–25 min vs Seminyak's 45 min). Recommend Jimbaran.

---

## Why this works for a toddler

- Four bases, but two of them are short stops (1–2 nights)
- Max 2 hrs of driving on any travel day
- Every day has a built-in nap window (12–3pm)
- Sanur final night = no panic morning of departure
- Mix of beach + culture + nature

## Day-by-day plan

### Day 1 — Arrive DPS → Jimbaran (~20 min)
- Pre-arranged airport pickup with car seat
- Check in, dinner at hotel or **seafood on Jimbaran Beach** (rows of fresh-grilled seafood at sunset, family-friendly, no party scene)
- Early sleep

### Day 2 — Uluwatu cliff temple + Nusa Dua beach
- **Morning:** Slow start, hotel breakfast, pool
- **Late morning:** Drive to **Nusa Dua beach** (~25 min) — calm, reef-protected, perfect for toddler swim
  - Or **Pandawa Beach** (less crowded, gorgeous limestone cliff entry)
- **12–3pm:** Lunch + nap (back at hotel)
- **4pm:** Drive to **Uluwatu Temple** (~20 min from Jimbaran)
  - 11th-century clifftop temple, 70m above ocean
  - Watch for monkeys grabbing sunglasses — keep tight on toddler
  - Sarong + sash provided at entry
- **6pm:** **Kecak fire dance at Uluwatu sunset amphitheater** — 1 hr, ends ~7pm. Open-air, dramatic, ocean backdrop. Doable with toddler if she's a chill evening type; skip if not
- **Late dinner:** back in Jimbaran (Menega Café or any beach warung)

> *Optional second cliff temple:* **Tanah Lot** is 1.5 hr north — possible Day 3 morning en route to Ubud, but it's a detour and crowded. Uluwatu alone is plenty.

### Day 3 — Jimbaran → Ubud (~1.5 hrs)
- Late-morning transfer after first nap
- Lunch at **Locavore To-Go** or **Cafe Pomegranate** (rice paddy views)
- Slow afternoon at homestay pool
- **Optional eve:** Walk Ubud Palace area, early dinner

### Day 4 — Ubud, swing + monkeys
- **Morning:** **Bali Swing** at Aloha Ubud Swing or Bali Swing Bongkasa
  - Toddler-friendly photo nests + small swings
  - Adults can do the big ones (helmet/harness included)
  - ~₹3,000–4,300 pp incl. transfers from many places; book on Klook
  - 2–3 hrs incl. drive
- **Lunch:** Hujan Locale or warung en route
- **12–3pm:** Nap
- **PM:** **Sacred Monkey Forest**
  - Hold tight to snacks, no earrings/sunglasses on toddler, carry near big males
- **Eve (optional):** 7:30pm Legong dance at Ubud Palace — 1 hr, leave anytime

### Day 5 — Ubud, gentle culture + rice terraces
- **Morning:** **Tegallalang or Tegenungan** — short walk through iconic emerald rice terraces (Tegallalang has the famous photos; Tegenungan is closer + has a small waterfall pool)
- **Late morning:** **ARMA Museum** (open-air, shaded) — Balinese painting in 1 hr. Skip if toddler tired.
- **Lunch:** Locavore To-Go or Warung Biah Biah
- **12–3pm:** Nap
- **PM:** Ubud market (handicrafts, fabrics) + Campuhan Ridge walk start
- **Dinner:** Warung Biah Biah or Bebek Tepi Sawah (duck in rice fields)

### Day 6 — Ubud → Sidemen (~2 hrs with one stop)
- **Stop:** Gunung Kawi viewpoint (skip the 300 stairs — view from top) OR skip entirely
- Sidemen by lunchtime
- Slow afternoon on the verandah; sunset over Mt Agung

### Day 7 — Sidemen culture-light
Pick **one**:
- **Option A (gentle, recommended):**
  - Morning: **Pelangi Weaving** workshop in Sidemen village (10 min)
  - Short flat rice-paddy walk near hotel
  - Long pool afternoon
- **Option B (bigger day):**
  - Driver to **Tenganan Bali Aga village** (~45 min) — pre-Hindu customs, double-ikat weaving
  - Lunch nearby, back to Sidemen for nap
  - Skip Tirta Gangga unless toddler is in a great mood

### Day 8 — Sidemen → Sanur (~2 hrs)
- Drive mid-morning
- **Sanur:** beach afternoon, last sunset, easy dinner
- Pack for early flight

### Day 9 — Departure
- Airport ~30 min from Sanur

## Health & safety

- **Hospitals:** Sanur — Bali Royal · Denpasar — Siloam, BIMC Nusa Dua · Ubud — Ubud Care Clinic
- **Pharmacies:** Guardian and Apotek Kimia Farma everywhere
- **Water:** Bottled only. Brush teeth with bottled. Ice in nice restaurants is fine
- **Spice:** Ask *"tidak pedas"* (not spicy). Safe staples: nasi putih, bubur ayam, mie goreng, banana, papaya, avocado
- **Sun:** Brutal 11am–3pm. Outdoor before 10 / after 4
- **Mosquitoes:** Worst at dusk. Long sleeves + repellent. Dengue is real — no preventive med, just avoid bites
