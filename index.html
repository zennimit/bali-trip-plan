Why end in the south, not the start? Landing at noon means you can drive straight up to the cool rice fields of Ubud the same day (1.5 hr). South Bali (Uluwatu, Nusa Dua) is 20 min from the airport — perfect for the last 2 nights so the flight home is a short hop. No Sanur stop needed.

Day 3 · Ubud Route in Maps

Tirta Empul & rice terraces

Holy spring purification, then Tegallalang
  • Early AMDrive to Pura Tirta Empul~40 min NE of Ubud. Go at opening (8 AM) before crowds and tour buses.
  • AMMelukat purification ritualThe famous holy-spring temple where you wear a green sarong and dip under 13 carved water spouts. Sarongs & lockers rented at entry (~₹50–100). Bring a change of clothes + flip-flops. Toddler can wade at the shallow edge with a parent — water is cool but not freezing.
  • Late AMTegallalang rice terraces15 min from Tirta Empul. Iconic emerald terraces. Coffee/coconut at Tegal Sari Cafe over the valley.
  • LunchBack to Ubud · Locavore To-Go or Warung Biah Biah
  • 12–3 PMNap
  • PMUbud market + Pura Taman Saraswati lotus templeFree to wander, beautiful for photos
  • DinnerSayuri Healing Food or SawobaliSayuri is fully vegetarian/vegan with eggs available. Sawobali does veg nasi campur in a rice-field setting.
About the green sarong. At Tirta Empul, devotees wear a green or white sarong over their clothes for melukat. Sarongs are provided/rented at entry. The ritual: enter the pool, work your way left to right under each of the 13 spouts, hands together, head bowed. Skip the last two (funerary cleansing only).

Day 5 · Sidemen Route in Maps

Culture-light · pick one

Don't try to do both
Option A — Gentle (recommended). Morning visit to Pelangi Weaving in Sidemen village (10 min). Short rice-paddy walk. Long pool afternoon.
Option B — Bigger day. Driver to Tenganan Bali Aga village (~45 min) — pre-Hindu customs, double-ikat weaving. Lunch nearby. Back for nap.

Driver & logistics

Getting around

One private driver for the whole trip. IDR 650–800k/day (~₹3,400–4,200), ~10 hrs incl. fuel. Same driver does transfers + day trips. Tip 10–15%.
  • Airport pickup with car seat — specify toddler 2.5y / ~13–15kg. Or bring your own.
  • Cash — ATMs reliable in Ubud + Sanur; carry some backup USD or INR.
  • Visa on arrival — ~₹3,000 (USD 35); passports valid 6+ months.
  • Travel insurance with medical evacuation — non-negotiable.

Health & safety

Good to know

  • Hospitals · Sanur: Bali Royal · Denpasar: Siloam, BIMC Nusa Dua · Ubud: Ubud Care Clinic.
  • Pharmacies · Guardian and Apotek Kimia Farma everywhere.
  • Water · Bottled only. Brush teeth with bottled.
  • Spice · Ask "tidak pedas" (not spicy). Safe staples: nasi putih, bubur ayam, mie goreng, banana, papaya, avocado.
  • Sun · Brutal 11 AM–3 PM. Outdoor before 10 / after 4.
  • Mosquitoes · Worst at dusk. Long sleeves + repellent.
